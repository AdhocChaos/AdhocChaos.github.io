Clean, accessible starter template

A modern baseline with an off-canvas side panel, a centered hero section, and alternating image/text cards that feel at home on mobile and desktop. Zero dependencies—just HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of JS.

Off-canvas Navigation

Tap the burger to reveal a tidy side panel of buttons. It’s keyboard- and screen reader-friendly, closes on overlay click or Esc, and restores focus to the trigger.

Alternating Cards

Cards automatically alternate image/text alignment on larger screens via :nth-child—no extra classes required. On mobile they stack gracefully.

Accessible, Modern, Lightweight

Thoughtful focus states, color contrast, reduced-motion support, and no external libraries—so you can drop this into any project and customize quickly.
